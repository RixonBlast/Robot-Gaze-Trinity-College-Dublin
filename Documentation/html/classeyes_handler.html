void smoothEyesMovement (int eyesState)
 This method allows to change the position of the eyes and the pupils by inputting an integer number between 0 and the value of nbEyesState - 1 . The movement will be smooth, therefore it will take time for the eyes to get to their final location on the robot's screen. The method activates the movement of the eyes and the Update method is operating the smooth movement. Note that inputting 0 will make the robot look on its right and inputting nbEyesState will make it look on its left.
