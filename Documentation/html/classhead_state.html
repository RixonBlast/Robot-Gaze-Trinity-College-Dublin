bool smoothHeadMovement (int headState)
 This method allows to change the position of the head by inputting an integer number between 0 and the value of nbHeadState - 1 . The movement will be smooth, therefore it will take time for the head to rotate to its final angle. The method activates the movement of the head and the Update method is operating the smooth movement. Note that inputting 0 will make the robot turn its head on its right and inputting nbHeadState - 1 will make it turn its head on its left.

 This methods allows to enable or disable the head movement. When the head movements are disabled the head won't be able to rotate using any of the two functions setHeadState or smoothHeadMovement, otherwise it will be possible.
